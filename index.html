rules

Press the UP key to rotate your tetromino, and LEFT and RIGHT to
move across the board. Pressing DOWN will speed up the tetromino. Fill up a row with no spaces to score points.
The game ends when the board is filled up with tetrominos, and the goal is to get as high a score as possible. The mini display to the top-right will show the upcoming tetromino.
